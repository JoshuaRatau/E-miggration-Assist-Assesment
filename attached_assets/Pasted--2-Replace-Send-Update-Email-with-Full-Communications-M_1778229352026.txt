## §2 — Replace “Send Update Email” with Full “Communications” Module

Replace the current top-right button labeled:

* “Send Update Email”

with a more advanced operational module called:

* “Communications”

This should no longer behave as a simple button action. It must open a dedicated communications workspace/panel/modal/page where administrators can manage all outbound client and lead communications across the CRM ecosystem.

The Communications module must support:

* campaigns
* bulk messaging
* onboarding communication
* system notifications
* promotional announcements
* operational alerts
* customer experience engagement
* reporting/history

The UI should feel similar to enterprise CRM communication centres found in:

* Zoho CRM
* HubSpot
* Salesforce

The Communications module should become one of the core operational modules of the platform.

---

## §3 — Communications Module Functional Requirements

Inside the Communications module, create the following operational sections:

### A. Campaigns

Enable mass campaigns for:

* subscription promotions
* onboarding campaigns
* new feature announcements
* educational immigration updates
* re-engagement campaigns
* product adoption campaigns

Campaigns must support:

* segmentation
* scheduled sending
* audience filtering
* preview before sending

---

### B. System Notifications

Allow administrators to send operational notices such as:

* scheduled maintenance
* service outages
* platform updates
* deployment notifications
* performance advisories
* security notices

---

### C. Draft Templates

Create reusable communication templates.

Required template categories:

* Promotional
* System Update
* New Feature
* Educational
* Customer Experience

Templates must support dynamic placeholders such as:

* {{first_name}}
* {{organization_name}}
* {{subscription_plan}}

---

### D. Communication Channels

Support:

* Email
* WhatsApp (future-ready architecture)
* In-app notifications

Email delivery should integrate with:

* Microsoft 365 Outlook
* [info@emigration-assist.com](mailto:info@emigration-assist.com)

---

### E. Communication History & Reporting

Track:

* messages sent
* delivery status
* open rates
* click-through rates
* failed deliveries
* campaign performance
* engagement analytics

Allow filtering by:

* date
* communication type
* campaign
* lead type
* delivery status

## §4 — Rename “Visa Type” Column to “Type of Enquiry”

The leads table currently uses:

* “Visa Type”

This is now too narrow because the system supports broader enquiry categories.

Rename the column to:

* “Type of Enquiry”

Supported enquiry categories should include:

* Overstay
* Declared Undesirable Valid Visa Holder
* First Time Entry
* Travel Assistance
* Immigration Consultation
* Professional Partnership
* Enterprise Demo Request

This field should work for both:

* B2C individual leads
* B2B professional/firm leads

## §12 — Responsiveness Requirements

Ensure the entire CRM dashboard is fully responsive and operational across:

* desktop
* tablet
* mobile

This includes:

* charts
* tooltips
* Kanban pipeline
* lead tables
* communications module
* filters
* buttons
* navigation

Specific requirements:

* tables should scroll correctly on smaller screens
* tooltips should not overflow off-screen
* charts should resize dynamically
* Kanban cards should drag properly on touch devices

The mobile experience must still feel premium and operationally usable.

## §13 — Final Product Objective

The completed platform should feel like:

“An enterprise-grade immigration CRM and operational intelligence platform with integrated communications, omnichannel engagement, subscription lifecycle management, lead intelligence, and polished SaaS operational workflows.”

The final system should:

* feel commercially deployable
* resemble mature CRM ecosystems
* support executive oversight
* support operational teams
* support marketing and communications
* support client lifecycle management
* support B2B and B2C workflows simultaneously

The platform must no longer feel like a prototype or internal tool.

It should feel like:

* a scalable SaaS product
* an operational command centre
* a premium immigration CRM ecosystem built for commercial rollout.
