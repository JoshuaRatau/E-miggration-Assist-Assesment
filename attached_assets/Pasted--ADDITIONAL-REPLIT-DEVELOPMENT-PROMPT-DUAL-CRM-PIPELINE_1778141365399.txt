# ADDITIONAL REPLIT DEVELOPMENT PROMPT — DUAL CRM PIPELINE SYSTEM, LEAD QUALIFICATION ENGINE & SUBSCRIPTION LIFECYCLE MANAGEMENT

Enhance the existing internal CRM and Lead Intelligence Platform for E-Migration Assist by implementing two fully structured CRM pipelines:

1. B2C Individual Lead Pipeline
2. B2B Professional Lead Pipeline

The system must support:

* automated qualification logic
* lifecycle tracking
* onboarding workflows
* subscription tracking
* lead scoring
* conversion analytics
* SaaS customer lifecycle visibility

The pipelines must visually and operationally resemble modern CRM systems such as:

* Zoho CRM
* monday.com
* SaaS customer lifecycle dashboards

The system must remain:

* highly visual
* enterprise-grade
* modular
* scalable
* operationally intelligent

---

# CORE OBJECTIVE

The platform currently receives B2C leads from:

[E-Migration Assist Self-Assessment Form](https://immigrationassist.replit.app/?utm_source=chatgpt.com)

These leads must now move through a structured SaaS conversion lifecycle.

Additionally, imported B2B professional leads must follow a separate commercial pipeline optimized for partnerships, onboarding, and enterprise sales engagement.

---

# 1. B2C INDIVIDUAL LEAD PIPELINE

Create a dedicated CRM pipeline for leads generated from the self-assessment form.

These leads represent:

* visa applicants
* travellers
* overstay appeal users
* immigration assistance users
* digital nomads
* travel support clients

The system must automatically assign these leads into the:
“Individual Leads Pipeline”

---

# B2C PIPELINE STAGES

Create the following CRM stages:

---

## STAGE 1 — NEW LEAD

Definition:
A user has completed and submitted the self-assessment form.

Trigger:

* successful form submission

Automatic actions:

* create lead record
* assign lead reference
* store assessment data
* add timestamp
* assign default status = New Lead

---

## STAGE 2 — QUALIFIED

Definition:
The system determines that the lead has meaningful commercial or onboarding potential.

This stage must support AUTOMATED QUALIFICATION LOGIC.

---

# AUTOMATED LEAD QUALIFICATION ENGINE (IMPORTANT)

Build an automated lead scoring and qualification system.

The system must automatically evaluate:

* assessment responses
* urgency
* visa category
* appeal complexity
* travel intent
* willingness to relocate
* WhatsApp availability
* engagement activity
* app interest

---

# QUALIFICATION RULES

Examples:

## High-value indicators:

* Overstay Appeal → high score
* Urgent immigration need → high score
* Corporate relocation inquiry → high score
* User selects “need assistance urgently”
* User engages multiple times
* User opens onboarding emails
* User clicks app download link

---

# AUTO-QUALIFICATION THRESHOLD

Example scoring:

| Criteria             | Score |
| -------------------- | ----- |
| Overstay appeal      | +25   |
| Urgent assistance    | +20   |
| WhatsApp available   | +5    |
| App download clicked | +15   |
| Multiple visits      | +10   |

If total score ≥ threshold:
→ automatically move to “Qualified”

---

# QUALIFICATION AUTOMATION REQUIREMENTS

The engine must:

* run automatically
* update lead scores dynamically
* support configurable rules
* log qualification reasons
* show qualification score on dashboard

---

## STAGE 3 — CONTACTED

Definition:
The system or operations team has initiated first engagement with the lead.

This stage should preferably be automated.

---

# CONTACTED STAGE AUTOMATION

When lead becomes:

* Qualified
  or
* manually marked for outreach

Automatically:

1. send welcome email
2. send onboarding introduction
3. provide app download link
4. log communication event
5. update lead status = Contacted

* welcome the lead

Include:

* app download links

* support contact details

## STAGE 4 — SUBSCRIBED

Definition:
The lead has:

* downloaded the app
* registered successfully
* subscribed to a paid package

The system must automatically move the lead to:
“Subscribed”

* account registration completes
  AND
