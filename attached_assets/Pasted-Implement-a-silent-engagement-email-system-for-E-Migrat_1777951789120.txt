Implement a silent engagement email system for E-Migration Assist (pre-launch mode).

GOAL:
Capture emails during assessment and send non-sales, informational updates to keep users engaged.

-------------------------------------

1. STORE EMAIL + CONSENT

Ensure prelaunch_leads table has:

- email (required)
- consent boolean (true/false)
- consent_timestamp

Consent checkbox text:

"I agree to receive updates about my assessment and platform availability."

Consent must be required before submission.

-------------------------------------

2. EMAIL INFRASTRUCTURE (USE RESEND)

Set up Resend integration:

- Send from: no-reply@eridetech.africa
- Environment variable: RESEND_API_KEY

-------------------------------------

3. EMAIL TYPES (NO SALES LANGUAGE)

A. Submission Confirmation Email

Trigger: After assessment completion

Subject:
"Your Assessment Has Been Received"

Body:

- Reference number
- Explanation:
  "Your information has been securely recorded."
- Reminder:
  "You can check your status anytime using your reference number."

NO:
- promises
- outcomes
- legal wording
- sales CTA

-------------------------------------

B. Passive Update Email (Manual trigger later)

Subject:
"Update on Your Assessment"

Body:

- General platform update
- Example:
  "We are currently preparing the full system to better assist cases like yours."

NO:
- booking links
- consultations
- payment prompts

-------------------------------------

4. EMAIL SAFETY RULES

Globally block:

- "approved"
- "rejected"
- "guaranteed"
- "you qualify"
- "we will fix"
- "Home Affairs will"

-------------------------------------

5. BACKEND EMAIL TRIGGER

On POST /api/leads:

After successful creation:

- Send confirmation email
- Log event in analytics_events:
  event_name: "email_sent_confirmation"

-------------------------------------

6. ADMIN EMAIL CONTROL (BASIC)

In /admin:

Add simple button:

"Send Update Email"

This:
- Sends batch email to all leads (or selected in future)
- Logs event:
  event_name: "email_sent_update"

-------------------------------------

7. FAILSAFE

If email fails:
- Do NOT block submission
- Log error silently

-------------------------------------

8. NO CTA RULE

Emails must NOT include:

- Apply now
- Book consultation
- Pay now
- Contact agent

-------------------------------------

OUTPUT:

- Email system fully wired
- Users receive confirmation email
- Admin can send silent updates
- Fully compliant with platform positioning
